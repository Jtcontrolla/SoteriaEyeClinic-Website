    What are the symptoms of glaucoma?

    With open-angle glaucoma, you may not notice any symptoms at all. You may have slight vision loss, but usually, your stronger eye makes up for it, so you don‚Äôt notice much of a change.

    In cases of closed-angle glaucoma, you may either have no symptoms or experience severe symptoms, such as:

    • Severely blurred vision
    • Excessive watering
    • Eye pain
    • Seeing halos around lights
