    This is our story

    Soteria Eye Clinic is a contemporary state-of-the-art Optometric and Optical Care Clinic. Centrally located in Lagos Nigeria, with operations in strategic geographical zones in the south, at Delta and Akwa-Ibom. Soteria optometrists and highly knowledgeable staff are committed to providing quality eye examination, along with impeccable treatment and services. The expertise at Soteria Clinic offers top-grade eye care. Including choice designer frames, contact lenses, and sunglasses. Soteria Eye Clinic was incorporated in 2014. We have since then recorded an upward trend, massive growth, and notable expansions. We progressively established the Delta Mall branch in 2017. Subsequently, Soteria Atlantic Centre Lagos, joined the group in 2020, playing a pivotal role in balancing out, our Lagos operations. We further expanded in the south, when we set up Soteria Eye Clinic at Uyo, in the year 2021. We also re-calibrated operations, with an administration office in the same year. Thus far, it has most certainly been a venture of great and unending fruition.
