    • Extreme sensitivity to light
    • Problems with night vision
    • Muted colors or yellowed vision

    The ophthalmologists at Soteria Eye Clinic often recommend corrective lenses, in the form of eyeglasses or contact lenses, to treat Cataract. Lenses normalize the refraction of light, making images clearer.
